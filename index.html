About Me

Hello, my name is Paul Kuhn. Ever since I was in kindergarten, I've always loved computers. I used to be a computer science major at Cabrillo College in Aptos, which is where I started my journey to becoming a programmer. I wasn't meshing well with the typical college lifestyle of taking classes all day and I had no clue how to study efficiently so I took some time off from school to develop myself. During that time, I switched majors, taught myself how to study efficiently, and became an accounting tutor for Cuyamaca College. I eventually stumbled upon a coding boot camp, which allowed me the chance to return to my coding roots in a style that is much more adherent to my preferences. I have recently found a passion in web development and I am elated to be able to share that excitement with the world!

My Work

A little about my work and style: I like to make sure that my work is, first and foremost, easy to follow, fix, edit, etc. I want something that is user-friendly. Websites that jump around all over the place, have improperly grouped elements, and have strange behaviors are not for me at all. The User Experience is paramount to a successful website--even if that website is for personal use. When the audience for the websites is satisfied then I am satisfied. Organization is key. I wasn't an accounting major for nothing! All my life I've organized things just for the fun of it. I remember organizing my old trading cards, my toy cars, etc, because it felt so satisfying. As far as design goes, I prefer a more minimalistic approach so that the website is not overwhelming.
